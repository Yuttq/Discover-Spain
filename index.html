Culture

Spanish culture is rich in tradition, music, and celebration.

Flamenco

Traditional Andalusian music and dance full of passion.

Bullfighting

Historic spectacle showcasing courage and tradition.

Festivals

Spain is known for its vibrant cultural and religious festivals.

Cuisine

Spanish cuisine is colorful, flavorful, and diverse.

Paella

A savory rice dish often made with seafood or meat.

Churros

Fried dough treats served with rich hot chocolate.

Tapas

Small plates that are perfect for sharing.

Barcelona

Home to Gaudí’s Sagrada Família and beautiful architecture.

Madrid

The capital city with royal heritage and lively streets.

Granada

The Alhambra palace reflects Spain's Moorish past.
